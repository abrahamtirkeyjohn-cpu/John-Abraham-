# WhatsApp Sharing Guide

## Sharing the Birthday Greeting Link
1. Open the browser on your device and navigate to the birthday greeting link.
2. Click on the link to open the greeting.
3. Use the share option provided by your browser or click the Share button on the greeting page.
4. Select WhatsApp from the sharing options.
5. Choose the contacts you want to send the greeting to, and then hit Send.

## Customizing Messages
- Before sharing the link, you can add a personalized message:
  - Tap on the message field in WhatsApp.
  - Write something special, such as "Check out this amazing birthday greeting!".
- You may also want to use emojis or GIFs to make your message more exciting.

## Deployment Options
- You can deploy your greeting on various platforms (like your website or a dedicated app) that allow you to generate a shareable link.
- Make sure that the link is accessible to anyone who has it, especially if they are not part of the same network or service.

## Pro Tips for Sharing on WhatsApp
1. **Use High-Quality Images**: Ensure that any images included in your greeting are high-quality to make a good impression.
2. **Engage with Your Audience**: Ask your contacts for feedback on the greeting or what they think of the customization options.
3. **Timing is Key**: Share the greeting link on or just before the birthday to enhance its impact.
4. **Group Sharing**: If you have a WhatsApp group with friends or family members, share the link there for a collective feel.
5. **Track Engagement**: If possible, track how many people interacted with the link to measure the success of your sharing!
